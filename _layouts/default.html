Learn More

Project description

HeKDisco proposes a novel knowledge discovery process in health care systems that will provide physicians with reliable evidence on various treatment stages and clinical events, thereby reducing individual clinical errors. HeKDisco, following evidence-based medicine (EBM), aims to use the best (reliable) evidence in making decisions about the care of individual patients so that clinician’s experience, patient’s values and preferences, and the best empirical clinical guidelines are integrated. HeKDisco aims to transfer big health data from volume-based to value-based by generating a relational knowledge base that can lead to innovative treatments, predict therapeutic outcomes, and early diagnosis.

HekDisco Pathology and Oncology Use Case

Objectives: Develop AI based diagnostic tools in pathology and a knowledge discovery platform to obtain the statistical relations between side effects and medications in oncology.

Expected Results: Oncology-based knowledge discovery platform to cut down time-to-decision of pathologists and oncologists as well as AI services to reduce the errors arising from diagnostic processes.

HekDisco Septic Shock Use Case

Objectives: Develop AI based diagnostic tools/services in the prognosis of Septic Shock in ICU. In line with this purpose, a knowledge discovery platform will be created in terms of not only classifying the diseases but also recommending alternative treatment solutions in order to prevent potential shocks in ICU.

Expected Results: A knowledge discovery platform to cut down time to decision of physicians as well as AI services not only to prognoses the Septic Shocks but also to minimize the potential errors/mistakes arising from diagnostic processes.


HekDisco Multiple Sclerosis Use Case

Objectives:

Istanbul University Neurology Department part: Build a reliable, clinical, technical and outcome based Real World Evidence database.

Belgian part: Knowledge discovery on MRI images in the follow-up of Multiple Sclerosis (MS) patients and exploration of the feasibility of using AI for modelling and then predicting clinical outcomes based on several endpoints of high relevance to MS disease progression.

Expected Results: RWE database for international scientific research and AI tools for MS assessing clinical value and economic impact.


HeKDisco for improving the care, diagnosis and treatment of elderly people

Objectives: Design and develop an AI-based platform to improve the quality of care for elderly people, focusing on Alzheimer's, high blood pressure, and diabetes, so that errors or failures in the treatment of patients are reduced over prescribed medications, multiple medication options, and the side effects of medications.

Expected Results: AI-based knowledge discovery platform increasing the quality of care for elderly people
